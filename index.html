Introduction

Welcome to DGA security System' Keypad tutorial.

This interactive tutorial will show you how arm and disarm your alaram system.

You can also follow these instruction to learn more advanced functions including how to bypass a zone, reset sensors, monitor a zone, and test your system.

Some keypad control multiple alaram systems. In these cases, your display will prompt you to specific wheather commands apply to specific system, or to all of them. Click the button below for all of the section.

Keypad tutorial

Yout DGA keyboard is ease to use. The green light at the upper left (POWER) tells you that your system has a power.

The red light beneath it (ARMED) comes on only when a system is armed, and the light goes out when you disarm it

Your display should show the time and will prompt you with instruction

In this tutorial, follow instruction by using your mouse and the keypad show here

Click the button below for the next section.

How to arm your alaram system

Follow instruction by using your mouse and keypad show here

  1. Press the CMD key untill the display reads: ARM DISARAM
  2. Press the key beneath ARM
  3. When the display ready ENTER CODE, enter your keypad code.(Use 1-2-3-4 for this tutotial)
  4. Next, finish by pressing CMD
  5. After the display counts down and the red ARMED lights goes on, your system is armed

    6. click the button below for the next section

How to disarm your alaram system

Follow instruction by using your mouse and keypad show here

Most ofter you will need to disarm your system after you enter your permises.

  1. Press the CMD key untill the display reads: ARM DISARAM
  2. Press the key beneath DISARM
  3. When the display ready ENTER CODE, enter your keypad code.(Use 1-2-3-4 for this tutotial)
  4. Next, finish by pressing CMD
  5. When the red ARMED light goes out, your system is disarmed

How to buypass a zone

Follow instructions by using your mouse and keypad shown here

Sometimes you may need to bypass a zone, for example when you need to arm your alaram system but can't get a window to properly close.

  1. To bypass a zone, press CMD untill ARM DISARM appears on the keypad display, then press CMD again for MENU
  2. Press the button beneath YES to access the menu, then enter your keypad code.(Use 1-2-3-4 for this tutotial)
  3. Press CMD to see the menu
  4. Keep pressing CMD until you reach BYPASS ZONES

    5. (If you skip past it, use your <- button to go back to it)

  5. Press any button beneath the display to select BYPASS ZONES
  6. Enter the zone you need to bypass.(Use 7 for this tutotial)
  7. Press the button beneath the BYP to bypass a zone 7
  8. Finish by pressing the <- key to exit the menu. (You can bypass upto three zones, but nerver priority zones like safes.)

    9. Before bypassing a zone, contact the monitoring center to confirm that part of your alaram system will be unarmed

How to reset your alaram system's sensors

Follow instructions by using your mouse and keypad shown here

To cancel zone bypasses and return your alaram system to its normal, disarmed status, reset all sensors.

  1. To reset sensors, press CMD untill ARM DISARM appears on the keypad display, then press CMD again for MENU
  2. Press the button beneath YES to access the menu, then enter your keypad code.(Use 1-2-3-4 for this tutotial)
  3. Press CMD to see the menu
  4. Press CMD again to reach SENSOR RESET
  5. Press any button beneath the display to reset your sensors
  6. Your sensors are reset and your alarm is now back to its normal, disarmed status.

    7. click the button below for the next section
