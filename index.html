Introduction

Welcome to DGA security System' Keypad tutorial.

This interactive tutorial will show you how arm and disarm your alaram system.

You can also follow these instruction to learn more advanced functions including how to bypass a zone, reset sensors, monitor a zone, and test your system.

Some keypad control multiple alaram systems. In these cases, your display will prompt you to specific wheather commands apply to specific system, or to all of them. Click the button below for all of the section.

Keypad tutorial

Yout DGA keyboard is ease to use. The green light at the upper left (POWER) tells you that your system has a power.

The red light beneath it (ARMED) comes on only when a system is armed, and the light goes out when you disarm it

Your display should show the time and will prompt you with instruction

In this tutorial, follow instruction by using your mouse and the keypad show here

Click the button below for the next section.

How to arm your alaram system

Follow instruction by using your mouse and keypad show here

  1. Press the CMD key untill the display reads: ARM DISARAM
  2. Press the key beneath ARM
  3. When the display ready ENTER CODE, enter your keypad code.(Use 1-2-3-4 for this tutotial)
  4. Next, finish by pressing CMD
  5. After the display counts down and the red ARMED lights goes on, your system is armed

How to disarm your alaram system

Follow instruction by using your mouse and keypad show here

Most ofter you will need to disarm your system after you enter your permises.

  1. Press the CMD key untill the display reads: ARM DISARAM
  2. Press the key beneath DISARM
  3. When the display ready ENTER CODE, enter your keypad code.(Use 1-2-3-4 for this tutotial)
  4. Next, finish by pressing CMD
  5. When the red ARMED light goes out, your system is disarmed
